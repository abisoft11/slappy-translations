Ladder tab

The Ladder tab lists all players in the currently active ladder, in descending order (best player at the top). The ranking is determined by the players' points and their ranking confidence.

Player details

Touching a player's avatar presents the player details page. On this page it is possible to view the player's current rating, their rating confidence and your expected result if you should play against them.

Arrange games

To send an invitation to arrange a game, players can touch the relevant opponent in the list on the Ladder tab, and select "Arrange game". Thereafter, the player should select the date and time to play and send the invitation. An optional message can be sent with the invitation. Invitations are sent to the opponent to be accepted/declined.

Invitations tab

The Invitations tab lists either current (outstanding) or all invitations that a player has sent or received. The colour of an invitation item in the list indicates its current status.

Capture results

Results can be captured either by touching a player in the Ladder list or by selecting a previously arranged game in the Invitations list. Captured results are sent to the opponent for confirmation.

Results tab

The Results tab lists a player's recent results. The colour of a result item in the list indicates its current status.

Ladder administration

Ladder administrators can manage the ladder settings, add new players and edit existing players' details.
