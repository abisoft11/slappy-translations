Slappy Subscription Terms of Use

The premium version of Slappy can be accessed by purchasing an auto-renewable subscription. Please note the following conditions of the subscription:

  • Once activated, the subscription entitles you to use all the premium features of the app. These features will remain available as long as the subscription is active.
  • Once activated, the subscription disables all advertising in the app. Ads will be enabled again if the subscription is cancelled, or the subscription fees are not paid.
  • Once activated, the subscription will automatically be renewed at the end of the current period, unless you cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • The cost of the subscription will depend on the subscription period.
  • You will be billed for the subscription once per subscription period, within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.
  • Should the cost of the subscription change, you will be notified, and can choose to cancel the subscription.
  • You can view and/or cancel your subscription at any time. You can access your subscriptions in your account settings.
  • If you cancel your subscription and later activate it again, you will be billed at the current subscription fee.
  • Promotional discounts will only be applicable to new subscribers, for the duration of the promotion.
