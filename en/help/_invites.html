Slappy Invitations

Players can optionally use Slappy to arrange a match in advance.

Sending invitations

To send an invitation to arrange a game, players can touch the relevant opponent in the list on the Ladder tab, and select “Arrange game”. Thereafter, the player should select the date and time to play and send the invitation. An optional message can be sent with the invitation.

If the ladder is a doubles ladder, the invitation will be sent to both of the competitor's players if they are registered.

After sending the invitation, it will be listed in the Invitations tab. The delivery status and status of the invitation will be updated when the opponent receives the invitation and replies to it.

Replying to invitations

When a player receives an invitation to play a game, it will be listed in their Invitations tab. The player can reply to the invitation by pressing the "Accept" or "Decline" buttons in the list, or by touching the invitation and pressing the appropriate button in the invitation details page.

If the ladder is a doubles ladder, either of the competitor's players can accept or decline the invitation.
