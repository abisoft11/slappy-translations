Slappy Sports Ladder

Slappy is a sports ladder management app. Players on the ladder can arrange games and capture results against other players, and monitor their progress on the ladder. Player ranking is done either using a points or a leapfrog system. Points and/or rankings are updated after each game, based on the result of the game and the current points or rankings of the 2 players. Slappy currently supports badminton, padel, racquetball, squash, table tennis and tennis.

Key features

  • Create and manage internal club ladders
  • Create personal ladders to compete with your friends
  • Arrange games in advance
  • Capture results
  • View historical results
  • Support for singles, doubles and mixed doubles
  • Ladder is updated automatically based on historic results
  • Player's rating confidence is used to determine how much their results affect their opponent's points
  • New players don't have large impact on opponents' current points until they become established
