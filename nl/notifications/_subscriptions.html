Subscriptions

Thank you for trying the Slappy Sports Ladder App. Slappy is free to use, but does come with some features that are only available to basic, premium or club subscribers. You have been given 30 days to try the premium features, after which you can choose to subscribe to either the basic, premium or club edition, or continue to use the free features.

The basic edition has the following benefits:

  • No ads - save on data costs.
  • Belong to multiple ladders.

Upgrading to the premium version has the following benefits:

  • No ads - save on data costs.
  • Belong to multiple ladders.
  • View other competitors' results.
  • View competitor rating change graphs.
  • Compare competitors and view results matrix.
  • View all recent results.
  • More to come in the near future.

The final option is club subscriptions. These give all club members premium access to the app at a fraction of the cost. Only a single club subscription is required for all club ladders.

Upgrade to the premium version before your trial period expires to receive a 20% discount. Upgrade to a club subscription now and save over 90%.
